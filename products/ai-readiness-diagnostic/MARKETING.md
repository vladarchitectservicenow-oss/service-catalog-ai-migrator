## ✅ The Solution

AI Readiness Diagnostic is a **ServiceNow scoped app** (`x_snc_ard`) that measures your instance's readiness for Otto/Now Assist:

| Feature | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| 🔍 **Process Debt Scanner** | Finds broken workflows, silent failures, orphan approvals → Process Health Score |
| 🗄️ **CMDB Health Analyzer** | Completeness, duplicates, orphans, staleness — per CI class |
| 📚 **KB Gap Analyzer** | Empty articles, outdated (>2yr), duplicates → Knowledge Readiness % |
| 🧪 **AI Simulator** | Dry-runs Otto/Now Assist against every workflow — finds failure points BEFORE go-live |
| 🗺️ **Roadmap Generator** | Prioritized fix plan: 30/90/180-day phases with effort estimates |
